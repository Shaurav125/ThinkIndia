About Us

vison

The Think India NIT Patna chapter engages in activities that contribute to nation-building. It fosters an environment where students can actively participate in discussions, debates, and initiatives related to various aspects of India’s growth and development. Through its programs, Think India aims to promote critical thinking, awareness, and social responsibility among students.

Misson

Think India envisions a future where young minds collaborate, innovate, and contribute to shaping India’s socio-economic landscape. It strives to create a platform for intellectual exchange, leadership development, and community service. By encouraging dialogue and action, Think India aims to empower students to become informed and responsible citizens
